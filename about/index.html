goodnight lavender ←

goodnight lavender is beauty breaking under pressure.

Born from the vision of multi-instrumentalist Kai van Deursen, goodnight lavender draws from emo, alt-rock, and punk to create something raw, restless, and unmistakably personal. With influences ranging from My Chemical Romance and Nine Inch Nails to The Pixies, the project walks a razor's edge between aggression and vulnerability.

Every album tells a story. "A Heart Lies Still" is a romance in decay, two lives unraveling beneath the grip of an unstoppable force. "Gallows" burns hotter and faster, a pop-punk fever dream about a rebel group called 7th UTOPIA fighting to outrun a fate already written.

The mission hasn't changed:
make music that understands you as much as you understand it.
